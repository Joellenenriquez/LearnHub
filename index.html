Our mission

LearnHub's mission is to inspire kids to love learning.

We believe the best way to do that is by linking learning to kids interests, connecting them with others who share those interests and giving them the autonomy to pick their own path. We make learning fun, social and self-directed.


Our Values

We believe all kids should be at the center of their learning.

We listen to kids, respect them and stand with them. We partner with kids, their families and educators to unlock opportunities for all kids.

In order to stand with learners, we all must be lifelong learners and nurture the kid inside.
